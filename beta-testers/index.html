Android beta

Join the Breaktime beta

Breaktime is now available on Android for beta testers. Thanks again for signing up to the waitlist and helping test an early release.

Become a tester Download Breaktime

Two months of Premium free

If you help test the Android beta, you will get two months of Breaktime Premium free. No card required. In the beta, Google Play will show the purchase as a test purchase using a test card, so you will not be charged.

What to do next

1

Use the invited Google account

Make sure Google Play is signed in with the same Google account that received your Breaktime beta invite.

2

Join the beta

Open the tester link and select Become a tester.

Join the Breaktime beta
3

Install Breaktime

After joining, click Download on the beta page, or open Breaktime directly in Google Play.

Open in Google Play
4

Start your free Premium test

Open Breaktime, go to the Upgrade to Premium screen, and tap Subscribe. Because you are a tester, Google Play should show that it is a test purchase and you will not be charged.

This is an early Android release, so bugs are expected. Please reach out if you have feedback, questions, or anything feels confusing.
